How to Order

To place an order, please send a DM with your desired shape, length, and design inspiration. Currently, I am based in Oxford and Cincinnati, OH, but shipping is available as well. You can reach me via Instagram at pressed.by.liz. I typically respond within 24 hours to confirm your custom measurements and provide a final price quote before I begin painting your unique set.
