Meet the Artist: Lizzie Braunstein

I started Pressed by Liz because I want to help people feel beautiful and put together without paying salon prices while still getting high-quality nails. I have been doing my own nails for the past six years and decided to turn this passion into a business for those who lack the time for long salon appointments. My goal is to provide a luxury experience that fits into your busy schedule and budget perfectly. I believe that everyone deserves to have nails that reflect their unique personality and style.

Every set I create is hand-painted with professional-grade gel products to ensure a salon-quality finish. Unlike mass-produced store brands, my press-ons are durable, custom-sized, and designed to be worn multiple times with the right care.
