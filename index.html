Sustainability & Quality

Because I use high-quality builder and color gels, these nails are incredibly sturdy and reusable. By gently buffing the dried glue off the back of the press-on after removal, you can easily rewear your favorite set for multiple occasions. This makes them a more sustainable and cost-effective choice compared to traditional acrylics that require harsh removal. You can switch your look in minutes without damaging your natural nail beds or spending hours in a chair.
